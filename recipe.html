Simple Omelette Recipe

A quick and easy dish, perfect for any meal. This classic omelette combines perfectly
cooked scrambled eggs, topped with your choice of cheese, vegetables or meat.

Preparation time

. Total:Approximately 10min.
. preparation:5min.
. Cooking:5min.

Ingredients

. 2-3 large eggs
. Salt,to taste
. Prepar, to taste
. 1 tablespoon of butter or oil
. Opyional fillings: cheese, diced vegetables,cooked meats,herbs

Instructions

1. Beat the eggs: In a bowl, beat the eggs with a pinch of salt and pepper until well combined.
You can add a tablespoon of water or milk for a thinner consistency.
2. Heat a frying pan: Place a non-stick frying pan over medium heat and add the butter or oil.
3. Cook the omelette: Once the butter has melted and bubbles are forming, pour in the eggs.
Tilt the pan to ensure the eggs cover the surface evenly.
4. Add the fillings (optional): When the eggs are starting to set on the edges but are still a little
runny in the middle, sprinkle your chosen fillings over one half of the omelet.
5. Fold and serve: While the omelette is cooking, carefully lift one end and fold it over the fillings.
Let it cook for another minute, then transfer to a plate.
